# 🌍 Price Hike: A Growing Concern

This repository contains my spoken presentation project for the course **Online Spoken English And Phonetics** (Batch: OFS-26-02) at MENTORS', instructed by **Maisha Suha**.

The presentation explores the causes, effects, and future outlook of global price hikes, with a special focus on Bangladesh.

## 📌 Project Overview

Price hike, also known as inflation, has become a major global economic issue. This presentation analyzes:

- The meaning of price hike (inflation)
- Global causes of rising prices
- Causes of inflation in Bangladesh
- Global and national effects
- Inflation statistics comparison
- Government measures and possible solutions
- Future economic outlook

The purpose of this project is to develop speaking skills while presenting an important real-world economic issue.
